Going to Boston

How to play the game.

  • Click the "Roll" button to roll the dice.
  • Click on a die you want to score and then roll the remaining dice.
  • Repeat.
  • Click the "End Turn" button to add the score to your round score.
  • Click the "End Round" button to add round wins to the scores.
